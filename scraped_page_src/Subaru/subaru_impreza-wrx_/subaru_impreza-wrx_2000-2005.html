Looking for a Subaru Impreza WRX near Washington?

The Subaru Impreza WRX has always been a winner due to its all-wheel drive, and the Impreza offers more go-anywhere capabilities than any of its competitors, while also offering more sure-footed performance in inclement weather conditions. The downside to all-wheel drive has classically been fuel economy, but Subaru has managed to sidestep that problem with its optional CVT automatic transmission, which can vary the gear ratios on the fly to maximize efficiency at all times.
